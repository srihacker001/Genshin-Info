The Dendro Archon - Nahida

Nahida, also known as the Dendro Archon and God of Wisdom, is the ruler of Sumeru. She is a young yet immensely powerful figure who embodies the essence of knowledge and nature.

As the Dendro Archon, Nahida governs over growth, intelligence, and balance. Though she may appear youthful, her wisdom and curiosity make her one of the most insightful Archons in Teyvat.

In the main storyline of Genshin Impact, Nahida plays a vital role in uniting the people of Sumeru and confronting hidden threats. Her powers are rooted in nature, dreams, and the collective consciousness of her people.

Trivia: Nahida is associated with dreams, purity, and learning. Her elemental burst allows her to read minds and influence enemies using the power of nature.
