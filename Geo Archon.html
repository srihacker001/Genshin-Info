The Geo Archon - Zhongli

Zhongli, also known as the Geo Archon and Rex Lapis, is the former ruler of the nation of Liyue. As the embodiment of the Geo element, he represents contracts, history, and tradition.

Known for his wisdom, elegance, and calm demeanor, Zhongli has lived for thousands of years and witnessed the rise of civilizations. Though he has retired from his duties as Archon, he still guides the people of Liyue in subtle ways under the guise of a mortal consultant for the Wangsheng Funeral Parlor.

His character and story explore the value of history, memory, and the importance of honoring promises. As one of the oldest gods in Teyvat, Zhongli commands immense power and respect.
