The Pyro Archon - Mavuika

Mavuika, also known as the Pyro Archon, is the deity ruling over the nation of Sumeru and the embodiment of the Pyro element. She is a fierce and passionate god who governs the principle of war, fire, and strength.

Mavuika is a highly revered figure in Genshin Impact's lore, often seen as a symbol of fire's intensity and fury. She is said to possess unmatched mastery over the Pyro element, and her power is capable of both destruction and rejuvenation.

Not much is known about her role in the main storyline, but it is expected that she will play a significant part in upcoming updates and future arcs of the game.

Trivia: As the Pyro Archon, Mavuika is often associated with the idea of perseverance and is said to never surrender in battle. Her followers are known to be strong warriors with an unyielding spirit.
