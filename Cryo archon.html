The Cryo Archon - Tsaritsa

The Tsaritsa, known as the Cryo Archon, rules over the nation of Snezhnaya. She is one of the most mysterious figures among the Seven Archons and is said to have a grand plan that involves reshaping the world of Teyvat.

Unlike other Archons, the Tsaritsa’s methods are more secretive and politically aggressive. She leads the Fatui, an influential organization that spreads across all nations, often acting under diplomatic disguise but with military power and intelligence gathering at its core.

Although her intentions are not fully known, it is hinted that her actions are rooted in a desire to protect the world from suffering, even if it means making harsh sacrifices. Her vision and motivations remain one of the biggest mysteries in Genshin Impact.

Trivia: The Tsaritsa is the only Archon who has not made a direct appearance in the game yet. Her Harbingers, including Childe and Arlecchino, act on her behalf throughout the storyline.
